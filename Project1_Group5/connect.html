How it Started: Harry & Harriet

Harry & Harriet, a dynamic couple with a passion for adventure, founded "Harry and Harriet's Hang Gliding Haven." Their journey into the world of extreme sports began when they discovered they shared a love for adrenaline-pumping actives. They decided to turn this enthusiasm into a business offering thrilling skydiving and hang gliding trips. Since its founding, The Haven has become a go-to destination for people seeking thrilling airborne experiences!

Harry and Harriet's 3 Favorite Things About the Haven:

  1. Helping Other People Make Lasting Memories
  2. Allowing People to See the World From a New Point of View
  3. Seeing the Excitement After People Fly for the First Time
