Our Services

At Harry and Harriet’s Hang-Gliding Haven, we offer a range of exhilarating services for adventure seekers including both solo experiences as well as guided tandem experiences. Explore this page to learn more about all the wonderful services we have to offer!

  • Skydiving: Experience the thrill of freefall with our professional skydiving instructors.
  • Hang-Gliding: Soar through the skies with our top-notch hang-gliding equipment.
  • Tandem Skydiving: Enjoy the ultimate skydiving experience with a certified tandem instructor.
  • Tandem Hang-Gliding: Share the joy of hang-gliding with a certified tandem instructor.

Skydiving

Experience the thrill of a lifetime with our skydiving adventures. Whether you're a seasoned jumper or a first-timer, our experienced instructors will ensure you have a safe and unforgettable experience. Solo skydiving is recommended for more experience divers and verification will be required to solo dive! You'll have the opportunity to jump out of a plane at 10,000 feet as part of a larger group. Our instructors are all professional certified and will provide an hour long course covering the essentials of sky-diving!

Hang-Gliding

Soar like a bird and take in breathtaking views as you hang-glide through the skies. Our hang-gliding services are world class and include a guided tour! Hang-gliding is a once in a lifetime experience which we highly recommended anyone to try at least once!

Tandem Skydiving

If you're new to skydiving, tandem skydiving is the perfect way to experience the adrenaline rush. For those of us who aren't comfortable or experienced enough to jump solo we offer a tandem experience so all can enjoy! You'll be securely attached to a certified instructor, ensuring a safe and enjoyable jump.

Tandem Hang-Gliding

Share the joy of hang-gliding with a friend or family member in tandem. It's an adventure you'll both cherish as you glide through the skies together with our experienced pilots. Tandem experiences offer a choice between partner rides and instructor guided rides. Both are amazing options and are guaranteed fun!
